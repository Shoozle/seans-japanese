Sentences and Phrases

Below are examples of sentences where I have attempted to mix Hiragana, Katakana and Kanji based on my learning so far. Click the Japanese text to hear it said out loud. There might be some mistakes.

こんにちわ,シオーンです

Hello, my name is Sean

私がねこだ

I am a cat

まいにちえいがおみます

Everyday I watch films

こんばんまんがおよみません

This evening, I will not read comic books

リアムとダンーが先生

Liam and Dan are teachers

おいしいからすしおたべます

Sushi is delicious, therefore, I will eat it

私わ英語と日本がはねせます

I can speak English and Japanese

お名前わ何ですか

What is your name?

これおください

Please may I have this?

すきですからおいしアイスクリームをかいます

I buy the delicious ice cream because I like it

どうしてこんばんみますか

Why will you watch it tonight?

かれあしてとこへーをのみますか

Would you like to get coffee with him tomorrow?

こけし

Kokeshi Dolls

あみだくじ

Amidakuji - Amida Lottery
