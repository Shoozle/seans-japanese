Sentences and Phrases

Below are examples of sentences where I have attempted to mix Hiragana, Katakana and Kanji based on my learning so far. There might be some mistakes. Phrases relate to Japanese culture that are singular words.

こんにちわ,シオーンです

Hello, my name is Sean

私がねこだ

I am a cat

まいにちえいがおみます

Everyday I watch films

こんばんまんがおよみません

This evening, I will not read comic books

リアムとダンーが先生

Liam and Dan are teachers

おいしいからすしおたべます

Sushi is delicious, therefore, I will eat it

私わ英語と日本ががはねせます

I can speak English and Japanese

お名前わ何ですか

What is your name?

これおください

Please may I have this?

こけし

Kokeshi Dolls

あみだくじ

Amidakuji - Amida Lottery
